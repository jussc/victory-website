Qualifications

At Victory Counseling, we understand how the therapeutic relationship between the client and the therapist has an important impact on the successful change a client experiences.

As a result, we have built a team of skilled clinicians that share a passion for providing high-quality therapeutic care in a compassionate and client-centered manner.



Therapy

If you're ready to begin a journey of change and self-discovery, we can provide support, guidance, and emotional safety to address the challenge you or your loved one are facing.

In addition to traditional counseling services, we offer specialized programs in the areas of: Autism Spectrum Disorder, Troubled teens and children, Family Conflict Therapy.



Sustainability

This is a place where you, or your child and family, can learn, grow and heal, individually or together. We offer a truly one-of-a-kind community-based treatment model that is the result of many years of experience in various settings.

Here, we teach skills that stay with our clients for the long haul. These are brought back into real-life situations and everyday relationships with long-term success.
